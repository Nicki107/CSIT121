Butter Chicken

  • chicken breast
  • jasmine rice
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tpsp minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cans of butter chicken sauce (Aldis has a great brand)

How to make:

  1. Clean your chicken, heat pan on stove top with olive oil, season chicken, cook chicken for 20 minutes.
  2. Add all ingridents above to chicken, turn heat to low for simmer.
  3. Let simmer for 20 minutes, let cool, then serve.
