Butter Chicken

Created by Chef Nikia

  • chicken breast
  • jasmine rice
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cans og butter chicken sauce (Aldis has a great brand)
  1. Clean your chicken, heat pan on stove top with olive oil, season chicken, cook chicken for 20 minutes.
  2. Add all ingridents above to chicken, turn heat to low for simmer.
  3. Let simmer for 20 minutes, let cool, then serve.

I Love this reciepe, its delicous and quick! I opt to not make my sacue from scratch but with the added ingredients you cant tell its not all from scratch.
